Request Account Deletion

We are sorry to see you go! If you wish to delete your StudentGig account and all associated data, you can do so by following the instructions below.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. In the App: Open the StudentGig app, navigate to Profile > Settings > Delete Account, and follow the on-screen prompts.
  2. Via Email: Send an email to nithin.v.kandula@gmail.com from the email address registered to your account with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".

Important Information

What happens when you delete your account?

  • Your profile, personal information, and login credentials will be permanently erased.
  • All your active job postings and applications will be removed from the platform.
  • Your transaction and message history will be deleted.

Please note: This action is irreversible. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

If you have any issues with this process, please contact our support team.
